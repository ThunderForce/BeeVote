Page elements

Plus button

At the bottom left corner of the page there is the plus button: it is needed to create groups, topics and proposals.

When you hover the cursor on it a different number of buttons will popup, according to the current page:

  • Green button: lets you create a group, will appear in any screen
  • Blue button: lets you create a topic, will appear in the group screen and in the topic screen
  • Yellow button: lets you create a proposal, will appear in the topic screen

Navbar

At the top of the page there is the navbar: it is a blue bar covering the entire screen width.

(Mobile) In the mobile view, the left icon is needed to open and close the sidebar.

There is a menu with various options, located differently according to your device:

  • (Desktop) In the desktop version the menu is a dropdown in the top left corner
  • (Mobile) In the mobile version the menu is located in the sidebar
The options are the following:
  • Feedback: redirects you to a Google form where you can give us feedback on the application
  • Report a bug: from here you can fill a form to report a bug you encountered
  • Edit profile: from here you can edit your profile and personal settings like name and language
  • Logout: click here to logout from the system

Actions

Create a group

To create a group:
  1. Hover on the red plus button in the bottom left corner
  2. When the popup buttons show up, click the green one
  3. Enter group information
  4. Click the "Create" button

Create a topic

To create a topic:
  1. Open the group in which you want to create the topic
  2. Hover on the red plus button in the bottom left corner
  3. When the popup buttons show up, click the blue one
  4. Enter topic information
  5. Click the "Create" button

Create a proposal

To create a proposal:
  1. Open the topic in which you want to create the proposal
  2. Hover on the red plus button in the bottom left corner
  3. When the popup buttons show up, click the yellow one
  4. Enter proposal information
  5. Click the "Create" button

Edit your profile

To edit your profile:

  1. (Desktop) In the desktop version, open the dropdown menu on the navbar

    or

    (Mobile) In the mobile version, open the sidebar from the navbar

  2. Click on "Edit profile"
  3. Change the settings that you need. You can change:
    • Your name
    • Your surname
    • Your profile picture
    • Your language (for now only english and italian are available)
  4. Click the "Edit" button
